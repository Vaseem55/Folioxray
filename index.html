Frequently asked

The questions we get most before someone uploads their CAS.

Is my CAS / portfolio data stored anywhere?

No. Your CAS PDF is processed in memory to extract holdings and is not written to disk or kept after your session ends. We never see or store your password beyond the single request used to decrypt the file.

Where does the holdings data come from?

From each fund's most recent monthly portfolio disclosure — the same SEBI-mandated filing used by Groww, ET Money and every other mutual fund platform. For exact current weightages, always cross-check on your fund house's website.

What exactly do I get for ₹99?

A one-time, full overlap report: every redundant fund pair, stock-level weightage comparisons across your entire portfolio, and a plain-language summary — generated once and unlocked permanently for that scan.

Is this investment advice?

No. FolioXRay is not a SEBI-registered Investment Adviser. Everything we show is factual portfolio analytics — overlap percentages, weightages, and redundancy — not a recommendation to buy, sell, or switch funds.

Which CAS formats are supported?

Detailed Consolidated Account Statements from CAMS or KFintech, password-protected (usually your PAN in capitals). International equity funds are currently excluded from the analysis.
